I. Boston Dynamics

Boston Dynamics is an american private enterprise located in Waltham in the Massachusetts state of the USA.


This enterprise wants to build advanced robots with the agility, dexterity, mobility, perception, intelligence and speed of a human or an animal. It is important to say that Boston Dynamics makes robots for both civil and military purpose. The Enterprise is now owned by the Hyundai Motor Group.


II. Atlas Basic Presentation

Atlas is a service robot, he is mainly made to assist in hazardous emergency situatiation.


Along the way to the final Atlas version, Boston Dynamics have made and plan to make lot of test with this robots and here is a list of some of them, that could help you more understand the kind of activity this robot will do.



ACTIVITYS EXAMPLES LIST

- Drive a utility vehicle at the site.

- Travel dismounted across rubble.

- Remove debris blocking an entryway.

- Open a door and enter a building.

- Climb an industrial ladder and traverse an industrial walkway.

- Use a tool to break through a concrete panel.

- Locate and close a valve near a leaking pipe.

- Connect a fire hose to a standpipe and turn on a valve.

III. A Few Mechanics Informations


OLDS EQUIPMENTS LIST

- Machined from billet.

- Unreliable external hose routing.

- High part count & bolt interfaces.

- Bulky multi-part box frame construction.

- An extern energy source.


NEW EQUIPMENTS LIST

- Aerial fluid routing with integral cooling.

- Hose-less fluid routing.

- Shell and Lattice structure for high strength/weight.

- Advanced Multi-material Interfaces.

- An intern energy source.

- Sensors in the: Head, Body, Legs, Feet and Pelvis.



Atlas is equipped with 28 hydraulic joints giving him the power to step up and carry things. An electric motor to make functionnal all the electric technology inside him and this without the external energy source. And at least a compact hydraulic power unit.
